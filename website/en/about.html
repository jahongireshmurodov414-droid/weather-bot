About Our Company

History, mission, and values of Ikhtiyor LLC

Over 10 years of experience in agriculture

Located in the picturesque city of Chirchik, Ikhtiyor LLC is one of the leading producers of agricultural products in the region. Our history began with a small family plot, which through hard work and the introduction of modern technologies has grown into a large agro-industrial enterprise.

Our Mission

To provide the residents and businesses of Uzbekistan with eco-friendly, fresh, and affordable high-quality vegetables.
